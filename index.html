British American Tobacco Bangladesh
Trade Marketing Intern | Fall 2018
Tangail, Bangladesh

The day I received my final grade from BRAC University, I got a phone call saying I have been selected for the Xcelerate Internship program of British American Tobacco Bangladesh. I was asked whether I was willing to go outside Dhaka to work with their trade marketing team. I agreed without any hesitation, as the trade marketing team of BATB was very well reputed. They are one of the best in the country. My posting was at a town called Tangail on the outskirts of Dhaka. I am forever grateful to my team, over there, for giving me a new perspective towards professionalism, confidence, and teamwork. In those three months away from home, not only did I learn the basics of trade marketing while working with a root level audience but also adaptability in a place with no known faces around.

New Age, Dhaka
Writer | May 2014 to May 2018
Dhaka, Bangladesh

I take immense pride in the fact that I come from a culturally aware family. I have uncles and aunts who are actively involved in the literary scene of our country. Even my own mother is a professor of Bangla literature. Therefore, since childhood, I nurtured a passion for writing and to give fruition to that passion I joined New Age in 2014. New Age is one of the biggest English newspaper in Bangladesh. Here, I basically worked on the Culture and Youth sections. Through New Age, I met several inspiring people, with stories to tell and I felt honored to portray their stories for the world.
